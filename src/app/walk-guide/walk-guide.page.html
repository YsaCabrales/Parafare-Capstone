Beware of Strangers

There is always a possibility that you will draw the unwanted attention of the criminal element. Always be wary of the people you come across. For added peace of mind, carry pepper spray or other protection devices.

Listening to music while walking is a wonderful way to utilize your time. But, keep the volume at a level where you can also hear your environment.
